About Me

I’m James Noble, an aspiring filmmaker and editor. Most of my work so far has been online, through YouTube commentary videos, game-focused edits and small animation projects. I am now focusing on building narrative and visual storytelling skills for film school.

I enjoy playing with pacing, humour and timing in edits, and I like mixing live-action, gameplay and animation together when it fits the idea. I am always trying to find new ways to make simple ideas feel more engaging.
